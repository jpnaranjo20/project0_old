Star Wars Main Characters

In this page you can find information regarding some of the main characters in the Star Wars Universe.

The information below is sorted according to appearance in a film or TV Show.

Films

Prequels (Episodes I - III)
  • Qui-Gon Jinn
    Jedi Master, mentor of Obi Wan-Kenobi.
  • Obi-Wan Kenobi
    At first Qui-Gon Jinn's padawan (appprentice), becomes a Jedi Master as the prequel trilogy advances. Eventually becomes Anakin Skywalker's master.
  • C-3PO
    A gold protocol droid, fulent in over 6 million forms of communication.
  • R2-D2
    An astromech droid capable of running ships and computers. Also, a formidable friend.
  • Chancellor Palpatine
    Chancellor of the Galactic Senate, secretly planning to overthrow the republic and form an empire. Also, secretly a sith lord by the name of Darth Sidious.
  • Darth Maul
    Darth Sidious' apprentice in the dark side.
  • Jango Fett
    A bounty hunter, who served as a template to build the republic's clone army.
  • Count Dooku
    Sith lord, also trained by Darth Sidious.
  • General Grievous
    A cyborg, part human, part droid, trained by Count Dooku in swordmanship skills. Not force-sensitive. Leader of the separatist army.
  • Padmé Amidala
    Former Queen of Naboo and senator in the Senate. Anakin Skywalker's love interest and future wife. Mother to Luke Skywalker and Leia Organa.
  • Anakin Skywalker
    Perhaps the most important character in the whole saga, Anakin is prophesied to be the Chose One to bring balance to the force. He begins as the most powerful Jedi Knight ever seen but is turned to the dark side by Darth Sidious and eventually becomes Darth Vader.
  • Mace Windu
    Jedi master, protector of peace in the galaxy. Has a purple lightsaber.
  • Master Yoda
    A very iconic character. A small and green Jedi master. Very wise in the force and very agile in lightsaber combat, serves as a master and guide to all Jedi.
Original Trilogy (Episodes IV - VI)
  • Luke Skywalker
    The son of Anakin Skywalker and Padmé Amidala, trained to become a Jedi by master Yoda seeks to bring balance to the force and to turn his father back to the light.
  • Princess Leia
    Princess of Alderaan and sister to Luke, becomes the leader and symbol of the Rebel Alliance against the Empire.
  • Han Solo
    Space smuggler who becomes involved in the rebel alliance and befriends Luke and Leia.
  • Chewbacca
    A wookie, Han Solos's best friend and partner in crime. Also befriends Luke and Leia and takes part in the rebel alliance.
  • Darth Vader
    The most feared sith lord in the galaxy, head of the Empire (along with Emperor Palpatine).
  • Grand Moff Tarkin
    Vader's most trusted general, head of the Death Star.
  • Emperor Palpatine
    Head of the Galactic Empire. Also known as the sith lord Darth Sidious.
  • Lando Calrissian
    One of Han Solo's old friends, who becomes involved in the rebel alliance.
  • Boba Fett
    A bounty hunter, son of Jango Fett.
  • Jabba the Hutt
    A criminal gangster with influence in politics and the criminal underworld, also featured in the prequel trilogy and in the TV Shows.
Sequel Trilogy (Episodes VII - IX)
  • Rey
    A scavenger with apparently no family who discovers herself to be force-sensitive and begins to search for the long lost Jedi master Luke Skywalker to find her identity and to train as a Jedi knight.
  • Finn
    A retired stormtrooper from the First Order who quickly befriends Rey and the Resistance in the fight against the First Order.
  • Poe Dameron
    General Leia's most skilled pilot of the Resistance.
  • Rose Tico
    A worker within the Resistance who befriends Finn and helps the Resistance escape from the First Order.
  • BB-8
    A sphercial droid with similar capabilites as R2 units.
  • Kylo Ren
    Also known as Ben Solo, he is the son of Han Solo and Leia Organa, former Jedi (trained by Luke) turned to the dark side by Supreme Leader Snoke waiting to claim revenge on Skywalker and rule the galaxy as head of the First Order.
  • Supreme Leader Snoke
    Head of the First Order, a force-sensitive sinsiter figure who trained Kylo Ren in the ways of the dark side.
  • General Hux
    The most importat ranked officer of the First Order.
  • Maz Kanata
    A mercenary and old friend of Han Solo, who knows most of the criminal underworld thanks to the cantina she runs on Takodana.

TV Shows

Star Wars: The Clone Wars
In this TV Show you can find the same exact characters as in the Prequel trilogy (as the show takes place between episodes II and III), as well as some other relevant characters such as:
  • Ahsoka Tano
    Anakin Skywallker's former padawan and skilled Jedi knight.
  • Commander Rex
    Commander of the clone army, loyal to the republic and very close to Ahsoka.
  • Asajj Ventress
    Assassin trained in the ways of the dark side by Count Dooku and hired to try and kill as much Jedi as possible.
  • Bo-Katan Kryze
    Mandalorian leader of a terrorist organization.
Star Wars Rebels
  • Ezra Bridger
    A kid who discrovers himslef as force-sensitive and trains to become a Jedi with the help of Kanan Jarrus.
  • Kanan Jarrus
    A Jedi knight who survived order 66 and agrees to train Ezra.
  • Hera Syndulla
    A Twi'lek woman, owner of The Ghost, a ship where she travels alongside Sabine, Zeb and Kanan beofre recruiting Ezra.
  • Sabine Wren
    A Mandalorian, part of the Ghost crew.
  • Zeb Orrelios
    A Lasat, part of the Ghost crew.
  • Chopper
    An astromech droid, similar to an R2 unit with similar capabilites, who has a funny personality.

Attention!

Most of the characters present in the original trilogy are also present in the sequel trilogy or even in Star Wars Rebels.

Relationship between characters is key!
